Green Commute is an IoT-based Vehicle Pollution Monitoring System developed to help reduce environmental pollution caused by personal vehicles. The project uses smart sensors and Internet of Things (IoT) technology to continuously monitor vehicle emissions in real time, allowing users to understand and manage their vehicle's environmental impact.

The system measures harmful pollutants such as Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Oxides (NOx), and Particulate Matter (PM) using sensors installed inside the vehicle. The collected data is transmitted securely to a cloud server, where users can monitor emission levels through a user-friendly web or mobile dashboard. Whenever emission levels exceed standard limits, the system generates instant maintenance alerts, encouraging users to service their vehicles and adopt eco-friendly driving habits. This proactive approach helps reduce harmful emissions while contributing to a cleaner and healthier environment.

Green Commute aims to raise public awareness about vehicle pollution by providing actionable insights and real-time environmental data, empowering individuals to make responsible decisions for a more sustainable future.
