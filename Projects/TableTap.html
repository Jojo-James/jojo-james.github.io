TableTap

Project information

  • Category: Web App
  • Client: BCA Sem 6 Project
  • Project date: 01 March, 2024
  • Tech Used: MERN Stack
  • Report: Click to view

TableTap is an intelligent tabletop food ordering system designed to modernize the restaurant dining experience through digital technology. The platform enables customers to browse an interactive menu, customize their meals, and place orders directly from their table using a tablet or mobile device, eliminating the need to wait for a server. Once an order is placed, it is instantly transmitted to the kitchen, ensuring real-time communication between customers, restaurant staff, and chefs. This streamlined workflow minimizes order delays, improves order accuracy, and enhances coordination between front-of-house and kitchen operations.

TableTap also integrates secure digital payment processing and provides restaurant owners with valuable analytics on customer preferences, order trends, and menu performance. These insights support informed business decisions, efficient inventory management, and improved customer service.

By combining automation, real-time communication, and data-driven decision-making, TableTap delivers a faster, smarter, and more personalized dining experience while significantly improving operational efficiency for restaurants.
