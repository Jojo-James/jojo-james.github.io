Clash of Nodes is a modern Android-based turn-based strategy game inspired by the classic Chain Reaction gameplay. Developed using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and WebView technology, the game combines native Android capabilities with an HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-powered game engine to deliver a smooth, engaging, and responsive gaming experience.

Players strategically place nodes on a grid, aiming to trigger explosive chain reactions that capture neighboring cells and eliminate opponents. Every move requires careful planning, making the gameplay highly competitive while remaining simple and accessible to players of all skill levels.The project follows a hybrid architecture where the Android application handles lifecycle management, native device features, and system integration, while the game engine is rendered inside a WebView. This approach provides excellent performance, scalability, and flexibility for future enhancements. Clash of Nodes is designed with future expansion in mind, including Firebase integration for authentication, cloud storage, multiplayer gameplay, leaderboards, and analytics, making it a production-ready foundation for a competitive multiplayer strategy game.
