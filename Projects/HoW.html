House Of Wears

Project information

  • Category: Website
  • Client: BCA Sem 3 Project
  • Project date: 01 March, 2022
  • Tech Used: HTML, CSS, PHP
  • Report: Click to view

House of Wears is a web-based clothing rental and retail management system designed to make fashion more accessible, affordable, and sustainable. The platform enables users to rent or purchase stylish clothing through a centralized digital marketplace, providing an economical alternative to traditional shopping while promoting the reuse of fashion.Built with an integrated database management system, House of Wears efficiently manages clothing inventory, customer information, rental transactions, and product sales. The centralized database ensures secure storage of information, maintains data consistency, and enables quick access to records for both customers and administrators.

The platform features an intuitive interface that allows users to browse, filter, and select clothing based on category, size, style, and budget. Administrators can manage inventory, monitor rentals, process returns, handle sales, and generate business reports through a comprehensive management dashboard.

By combining e-commerce functionality with rental services, House of Wears encourages sustainable fashion while helping users enjoy premium clothing without the high cost of ownership or long-term storage.
